Adds various craftable tents. When in use you will respawn at a tent intead of a bed, but it does not override your bed's spawn point when you enter the world.

[i/s1:184] Set up temporary spawn points by crafting tents!
[i/s1:184] Spawn at a tent instead of a bed whilst active
[i/s1:184] Various tiers of tents

As this was ported over from WeaponOut, any tents picked up or crafted from WeaponOut are converted to this mod's version.
